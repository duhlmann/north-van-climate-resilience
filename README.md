# North Vancouver Climate Resilience Score

### Goal
Score every neighbourhood in the **District of North Vancouver** on climate resilience using open data and a transparent scoring system.  
The final results will be displayed on an interactive **Streamlit dashboard** with maps, rankings, and recommendations.

---

### What contributes to "climate resilience"?
- Heat exposure (tree canopy, cooling centres, surface temperature)
- Flood and landslide risk
- Wildfire interface exposure
- Social vulnerability (seniors, low income, housing type)
- Park and green space access
